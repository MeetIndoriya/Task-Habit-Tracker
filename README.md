# Habit & Task Tracker
### Your Personal Progress Command Center

> A powerful, all-in-one habit and task tracking system designed to help you build consistency, break bad patterns, and visualize your growth over time.

---

## Overview

Track every habit you care about — from morning workouts to meditation, hydration, reading, and more — across a fully interactive monthly grid. Log each day as **Done ✓**, **Missed ✗**, or **Skipped ~** with a single click, and watch your progress come to life through color-coded cells, heatmap calendars, and real-time charts.

---

## Features

### 📊 Monthly Tracker Grid
See all your habits and every day of the month in one clean view. Click any cell to instantly cycle through your daily log status.

| Status | Symbol | Color |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Done | ✓ | 🟢 Green |
| Missed | ✗ | 🔴 Red |
| Skipped | ~ | 🟡 Amber |
| Not logged | — | ⬜ Empty |

---

### 🔥 Streak System
Stay motivated with live streak counters that reward consistency.

- **Current Streak** — Consecutive days where all scheduled habits were completed
- **Longest Streak** — Your all-time personal best record
- **Per-habit Streak** — Individual streak shown next to each habit row

---

### 📈 Progress Charts
Three visual bar charts give you a complete picture of your performance, each bar color-coded by completion rate:

- 🟢 **≥ 80%** — Green (Excellent)
- 🟡 **≥ 50%** — Amber (Good)
- 🔴 **< 50%** — Red (Needs Improvement)

| Chart | What It Shows |
|-------|---------------|
| Daily | Completion % for each day of the selected month |
| Weekly | Completion % across the last 8 weeks |
| Monthly | Completion % across the last 12 months |

---

### 🧭 Smart Dashboard
Four live metric cards update in real time as you log your habits:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Active Habits | Total number of habits currently being tracked |
| Completion Rate | Percentage of scheduled habits completed this month |
| Current Streak | Consecutive fully-completed days in a row |
| Longest Streak | Best streak ever achieved |

---

### ➕ Habit Builder
Add, edit, or delete habits with full customization:

- **Name** — Give your habit a clear, meaningful title
- **Description** — Add a short note or goal
- **Icon** — Choose from 12 unique symbols (◉ ★ ◆ ▲ ● ◐ ◈ ♫ ✏ ◑ ♥ ◇)
- **Color** — Pick from 12 colors to personalize each habit
- **Active Days** — Select which days of the week the habit applies
- **Reminder Time** — Set an optional daily reminder

---

### 📋 Progress Summary
Click the **Summary** button to generate a full monthly report:

- Total Done ✓, Missed ✗, and Skipped ~ counts
- Overall completion rate with a visual progress bar
- Per-habit breakdown with individual success rates
- Motivational message based on your performance

---

### 🗓️ Heatmap Calendar
A color-coded calendar overlays your monthly tracker:

- 🟢 **Green** — High completion day (≥ 80%)
- 🟡 **Amber** — Partial completion day (≥ 50%)
- 🔴 **Light Red** — Low completion day (< 50%)
- 🔵 **Purple Highlight** — Today's date

---

### 📅 Multi-Year Navigation
Navigate your habits from **January 2025** to **December 2030** using simple year and month dropdowns — perfect for long-term tracking and reviewing past progress.

---

## How to Use

1. **Open the app** in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. **View your tracker** — the current month loads automatically
3. **Click any cell** in the grid to log a habit as Done → Missed → Skipped → Clear
4. **Add a new habit** via the Manage Habits tab → click **+ Add Habit**
5. **Check your progress** by switching to the Charts tab
6. **Generate a summary** by clicking the **Summary** button at the top right
7. **Navigate months** using the Year and Month dropdowns

---

## Status Reference

```
✓  Done     — Habit completed successfully
✗  Missed   — Habit was not completed
~  Skipped  — Habit was intentionally skipped
—  Empty    — No log entry for this day
◆  Streak   — Consecutive days completed (shown per habit)
```

## Tips for Success

- **Log daily** — take 30 seconds each evening to update your tracker
- **Start small** — add 2–3 habits first, then grow gradually
- **Use streaks** — let the streak counter motivate you to not break the chain
- **Review monthly** — use the Summary button at month end to reflect on patterns
- **Customize colors** — assign meaningful colors to habit categories for quick scanning
